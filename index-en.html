Rune of Fire

The Fire Rune, obtainable in the volcano, grants the ability to light your immediate surroundings. By activating this rune, an intense light spreads around you, revealing the darkness and aiding your exploration in shadowy areas. This mechanic allows you to better navigate hostile environments, providing clear vision even in the darkest places of the game.

Rune of Air

The Air Rune, obtainable on the floating island, allows you to move objects from a distance by manipulating the air currents. By activating this rune, you can control the wind to move environmental elements, solve puzzles, or interact with objects remotely. This ability offers great freedom of action and a new way to explore hard-to-reach areas.
